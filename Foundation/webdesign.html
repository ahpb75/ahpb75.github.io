Introducing | Sensofec Business Command Sites
All your business, located in one secure place.

Sensofec Business Command websites are tailored to bring all the things your business uses the internet for into one central hub. Business e-mail, client management, data analysis, camera feeds - all hosted on your site in an encrypted server-side application.

Your Main Website integrated with Business Management Tools

It's as simple as adding a "manage.yoursite.com" subdomain to your site, or an extra page. Just like banks have online banking, we enable you to perform highly efficient online business through your own website.

Turnkey Solutions Available

You can opt for our turnkey package that adds the subdomain to your site with the specific features you want. The application bears your logo, your ownership, and functions exactly to your specifications.

Own the copyright with our From Scratch Solution

Depending on the complexity of your business interactions on the web, your design may be eligible for a patent or copyright. Your business will then own the rights to your custom application.
